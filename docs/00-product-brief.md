# Product Brief

## One Line

JP LMS VibeOps is a Japanese university AI Learning Operations OS that turns LMS activity into explainable AI decisions, instructor-approved interventions, and measured pilot outcomes.

## What We Are Building

We are building an operating layer for learning operations, not another LMS screen pack.

The system observes learning events from existing university platforms, produces explainable AI cards, routes high-impact actions through approval gates, measures whether interventions worked, and keeps the privacy/governance trail required for a Japanese university pilot.

## Primary Buyers And Users

| Actor | Job |
| --- | --- |
| NetLearning executive | Decide whether this can become a differentiated AI layer without replacing the existing platform |
| University executive | Approve a low-risk pilot with measurable outcomes |
| Faculty member | Understand where students struggle and approve targeted improvements |
| Student | Receive timely support without being labelled or surveilled |
| Academic affairs / IT / legal | Confirm APPI, consent, audit, integration, and data-minimization readiness |

## Core Product Promise

Current AI LMS proposals often stop at recommendations. JP LMS VibeOps must go further:

1. Detect a learning operations signal.
2. Explain why the signal matters.
3. Recommend a bounded action.
4. Require the right human approval.
5. Execute through existing LMS surfaces.
6. Measure outcome after a fixed interval.
7. Feed the result back into the next decision.

## MVP Scope

The MVP should prove one closed loop:

Student lecture struggle signal -> instructor decision queue -> course co-creation draft -> approved release -> two-week effect measurement -> impact ledger.

This loop maps directly to the strongest existing material while avoiding a broad, fragile demo.

## Non Goals

- Replacing NetLearning, manaba, or campus LMS identity systems.
- Showing a live autonomous agent that can mutate production course content.
- Building a generic chatbot-first LMS.
- Claiming predictive accuracy without PPV, calibration, uncertainty, and outcome measurement.
- Using "risk list" UX as the primary instructor experience.

## Differentiation

The product is differentiated by the combination of:

- Explainable AI cards with mandatory 5-part structure.
- Approval-gated learning operations.
- Japanese university cultural and legal fit.
- Measured intervention effects rather than prediction-only dashboards.
- VibeOps-style run records, review packets, and rollback discipline.
